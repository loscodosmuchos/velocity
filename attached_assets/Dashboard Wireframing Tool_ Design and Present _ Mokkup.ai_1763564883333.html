About the dashboard

Supplier management has been critical for modern businesses to maintain their top line and increase stability. Most of the working capital is used to procure goods and services, making it vital for every business to focus on strategic procurement to achieve the best product reliability, quality, and effectiveness. 

The Supplier dashboard template monitors the performance of the suppliers and tries to identify the most efficient suppliers while reducing over-dependence:

  • Increase revenue by reducing cost
  • Identifying the maverick suppliers
  • Evaluating the suppliers for selection
  • Comparing the existing suppliers currently employed

The values corresponding to the above goals are measured across all regions and product categories, making it critical for planning the future marketing strategies of the company. 

Dashboard Template Insights

The Supplier Dashboard wireframe provides key insights to enhance supplier management and optimize procurement processes:

  • Strategic supplier management is essential for businesses to optimize costs, improve product reliability, and stay competitive in the market.
  • A focus on procurement efficiency allows the dashboard to monitor supplier performance, helping businesses identify efficient suppliers to reduce costs and boost revenue.
  • Mitigating Maverick Spending ensures that businesses adhere to procurement policies, optimizing their expenditure and preventing unauthorized spending.
  • Supplier evaluation and selection enables businesses to assess current suppliers, compare performance, and make informed decisions about supplier selection and retention.
  • A comprehensive performance analysis through category views allows stakeholders to examine procurement cycle time, expenditure trends, and supplier distribution, supporting strategic supplier management.
