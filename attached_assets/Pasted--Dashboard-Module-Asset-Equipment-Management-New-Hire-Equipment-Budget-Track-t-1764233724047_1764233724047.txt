**Dashboard Module: Asset & Equipment Management**

* **New-Hire Equipment Budget**

  * Track the \$8.6 M allocation over 4 years for outfitting new hires.
  * Display – total committed vs. spent to-date; forecast remaining funds.
* **Contract & Change-Order Repository**

  * Central database for master service agreements (SW/PDF) and all amendments.
  * Submit, review, approve change-order requests in-app; log timestamps & approver.
* **Monthly Payment Schedule**

  * Tile showing guaranteed monthly payment amount, due dates, and status.
  * Trend chart – actual vs. planned payments; highlight variances.
* **Spend Threshold Alerts**

  * Trigger notifications when spend reaches configurable % of budget (e.g., 80%).
  * Banner or blinking alert if change-order approval is overdue.

**Module: Time & Materials Invoicing**

* **End-to-End Workflow Tracker**

  1. Contractor submits T & M entries.
  2. Hyundai management approval.
  3. Export to Balance Staffing for modifications.
  4. Re-export back to Hyundai’s ERP.
* **Audit Trail Dashboard**

  * Table with status per invoice: “Submitted,” “Approved,” “Modified,” “Re-exported.”
  * Filter by date, contractor, PO, or approval lag.
* **KPI Cards**

  * Avg. approval turnaround time.
  * Number of modifications per period.
  * % of invoices overdue.
* **Approval-Delay Alerts**

  * Color-coded indicators for invoices pending > X days.
  * Option to escalate to next-level approver.

---

**Contextual Notes for “Wes-Procurement”**

* **User Persona**: Simple UI – clear panels, minimal clicks; focused on core tasks.
* **Business-Unit Scale**: \$100 M IT spend; high visibility on budgets and approvals.
* **Pain Points**:

  * Fragmented systems ? lost or delayed data.
  * Manual PDF/SW tracking ? no real-time visibility.
  * Complex T & M handoffs ? transcription errors.
