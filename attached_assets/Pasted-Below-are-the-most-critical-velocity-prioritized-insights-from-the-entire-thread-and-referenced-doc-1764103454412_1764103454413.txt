  - Unified resource visibility across all sources (ATS, VMS, monitoring tools, timesheets)
  - Automated end-to-end data flow and AI-powered process triggers, eliminating spreadsheet/powerpoint chaos for project, resource, and compliance management
  - 40% cost reduction and 30-50% cycle time improvement, validated by industry benchmarks[2][1]
- **Agentic/AI-native startups** offer cutting-edge predictive analytics, conversational interfaces, and flexible integration with enterprise infrastructure, whereas SaaS giants (e.g., Salesforce, Oracle, SAP) are optimized for their ecosystem, move slower on innovation, and often lack true low-code/no-code extensibility.[3][1]
